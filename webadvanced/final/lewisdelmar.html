Lewis Del Mar

Not a guy but rather two guys, Lewis Del Mar is a New York based electro-acoustic indie pop duo made up of Washington, D.C. transplants Danny Miller (singer/songwriter) and Max Harwood (drummer/producer). Having collaborated on and off since grade school, they formed the group in 2013 after relocating to Rockaway Beach, and the project after their fathers (both names Lewis) and their costal locale. Melodic and percussion-driven, and incorporating samples and manipulated sound, their sleek debut single, "Loud(y)," went viral in 2015, topping Hype Machine's Popular Tracks chart.
