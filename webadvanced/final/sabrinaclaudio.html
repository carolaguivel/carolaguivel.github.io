Sabrina Claudio

Rising R&B songstress Sabrina Claudio recently released her latest project, the 8-track collection, No Rain, No Flowers. Sabrina has garnered attention from fans and critics alice since independently unveiling her debut EP Confidently Lost in 2016 on Soundcloud. The EP quickly amassed over 3 million cumulative plays and upon commercial release, shot to No. 3 on the iTunes R&B chart sitting behind Khalid and The Weeknd. This was only the beginning for the half Puerto Rican, half Cuban artist whose breathy vocals immediately hypnotize and captivate. Sabrina would follow Confidently Lost with About Time, which shot to the top of the R&B charts.
